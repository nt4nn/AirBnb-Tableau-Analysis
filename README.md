# Airbnb Data Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)

## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive data analysis of Airbnb listings using Tableau. The goal is to uncover key patterns in pricing, demand, host activity, and customer preferences across different neighbourhoods.
The dashboard enables users to explore how factors such as location, room type, reviews, and availability influence listing performance and pricing strategies.

## Objectives
* Analyze pricing variations across room types and locations
* Identify high-demand neighbourhoods and listing trends
* Evaluate host performance and market concentration
* Understand the relationship between reviews, availability, and bookings

## Key Insights

### Pricing Trends
* Entire homes are priced **2–3x higher** than private rooms
* Shared rooms are **50–70% cheaper**, catering to budget users
* Property type is the strongest driver of price variation

### Location Analysis
* Top neighbourhoods contribute to **40–60% of total demand**
* Listings in prime areas are priced **30–80% higher**
* Location significantly impacts both price and occupancy

### Demand & Reviews
* Listings with high reviews receive **2x–4x more bookings**
* Reviews act as a strong indicator of trust and popularity

### Host Insights
* Top hosts (10–20%) manage multiple listings and generate **~50%+ of activity**
* Indicates the presence of professional hosts dominating the market

### Availability Patterns
* Highly booked listings show **low availability (<100 days/year)**
* Suggests a negative correlation between availability and demand

### Customer Preferences
* Tourist areas favor **entire homes (70%+)**
* Residential areas show higher demand for **private rooms (50%+)**

## Business Implications
* Pricing strategies should align with **location and property type**
* Increasing early reviews can boost bookings by **up to 2x**
* Hosts can optimize occupancy by matching listing types to local demand
